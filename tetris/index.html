Basic Tetris

Hello and welcome to my basic Tetris game, which I programmed myself. (Well, actually, I used a Tutorial.)

Like I said, this version of Tetris is very simple, but it still has colours, a few keyboard controls and more. The controls are very simple, but they are still listed below. The Tetrominoes are controlled using the arrow, WASD, keypad or IJKL keys (just in case you are left-handed and your arrow keys are as small as mine). Rotating the Tetromino left happens by pressing M, Q or U.
Warning: There is a Software bug, which, when you resume the game, resets the next Tetromino and selects a new one. In addition, you cannot restart the game by pressing the restart button, unless you reload the tab. If you find a way to fix it, please don't hesitate to contact me via mail.

Key Functions
Key Function
Esc(ape)/ 7 Play/ pause
M/ Q/ U Rotate left
Upper arrow/ W/ I/ 8 Rotate right
Left arrow/ A/ J/ 4 Move left
Lower arrow/ S/ K/ 5 Drop
Right arrow/ D/ L/ 6 Move right
